Welcome to SaleSniper

SaleSniper is a powerful and easy to use mobile app to help you get more sales. You get:

  • An Android app that can be installed free of charge (for up to 5 sales personnel). The app helps your sales agents search for and find prospects/businesses near them using a simple interface like Google Maps.
  • A web portal that allows the supervisor or employer to see all relevant information, including: the location and contact details of prospects, exactly where your sales staff are, where they have visited, how long they spent at each prospect location, etc.
  • Powerful and easy-to-understand reporting interface.
